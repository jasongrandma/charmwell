Pricing Guide

What Affects Cost

  • Complexity: Multi-function items require more enchantment layers
  • Potency: Stronger effects demand rarer materials
  • Duration: Permanent enchantments cost more than temporary ones
  • Materials: Precious metals and gems hold magic better
  • Rarity: Uncommon magical components increase price

Expensive to Craft

  • Items affecting life force or soul
  • Time manipulation enchantments
  • Dimensional or teleportation magic
  • Permanent transformation effects
  • Weather or large-scale influence

More Affordable

  • Simple protection wards
  • Light and warmth charms
  • Minor healing acceleration
  • Detection and sensing trinkets
  • Luck and fortune tokens

Timeline

Most custom orders take 2-4 weeks to complete. Complex or legendary items may require additional time for sourcing rare materials and careful enchantment.
