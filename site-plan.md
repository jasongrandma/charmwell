# Charmwell - Site plan

## Business Overview

Charmwell is an arcane shop selling charms, wards, spells, magical objects, and knick-knacks from a humble hillhouse in a place called Ornaldo. We collect, trade, create and sell arcane items. We will take custom made order requests and create objects of a specific nature.

**Target audience:** Mystic individuals interested in physical magical tokens designed for any purpose. All ages

**Brand personality** Helpful, kind, mystical, natural. Jack-of-all trades kind of business.

## Page Structure

### 1 Home

- Hero section that says "Aidful arcana abundant"
- Charms, trinkets, items, objects of all sorts are available.
- Inquiry to custom-made orders.
- Feel free to come visit in person and view the entire collection.

### 2 Charms

- Sections labeling kinds of charms like healing, clairvoyant, dangerous, utility, etc.
- Images of charms with brief details.
- Rarity levels for each item.

### 3 Custom orders

- Form to request specifically made orders detailing form, function, magic type, budget.
- Details and guidelines about what kinds of objects are more expensive to make.

### 4 Contact

- Humble hillhouse location in Ornaldo and hours
- Form for inquiries and questions.
- Map placeholder

## Design Direction

**Colors:**

- Primary: Mystical purple (#574571)
- Secondary: Beige (#EDE8D0)
- Accent: Forrest green (#374f2f)

**Typography**

-Headings: Old style serif font. Old looking and official.
-Body: Rounded sans serif. Plain and simple.

**Overall aesthetic:** Humble, cozy, inviting, plentiful but legible.
